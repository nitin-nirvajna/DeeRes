Machine Learning Simplified

The difference between traditional programming and learning from examples

Traditional Programming

STOP

If shape is octagon AND color is red AND contains "STOP", then it's a stop sign.

Limitations:

Fails when sign is faded or at an angle

Must be explicitly told what to look for

Cannot adapt to new situations

VS

Machine Learning

Stop Sign

Not Stop Sign

Not Stop Sign

Show thousands of images, labeling which are stop signs and which are not.

Benefits:

Learns underlying patterns on its own

Robust against variations (faded signs, angles)

Creates its own rules and adapts

Machine Learning is like teaching by example rather than giving a rigid instruction manual
