AI's Core Concept

Systems designed to perceive, reason, and act by learning

AI is the science of creating intelligent machines that perform tasks requiring human intelligence.

Traditional Programming

  • Explicit rules for every scenario
  • Stop sign example: IF shape=octagon AND color=red THEN stop_sign=True
  • Fails when sign is faded or at angle
VS

AI Programming

  • Provide examples and labels
  • Show computer thousands of stop sign and non-stop sign images
  • AI creates its own rules based on patterns

Key insight: AI learns by example, like teaching a child to identify a stop sign.
