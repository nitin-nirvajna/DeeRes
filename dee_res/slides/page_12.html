Ethical Considerations

Addressing the human dimensions of artificial intelligence

Algorithmic Bias

AI systems learn from the data they're given.

If this data reflects existing societal biases (e.g., in hiring or loan data), the AI can learn and amplify these prejudices.

This leads to unfair outcomes that perpetuate inequality.

Privacy & Data Security

AI models require massive amounts of personal data.

Who has access to our data and how it's used raises significant concerns.

Questions about data ownership and protection from misuse become critical.

Misinformation & Deepfakes

Generative AI can create highly realistic but fake content.

"Deepfakes" enable the creation of convincing fake images, videos, and audio.

This poses a serious threat by enabling rapid spread of convincing misinformation and propaganda.

"Ethics is about building AI that reflects our values and benefits society."
