The Genesis of Intelligence

The birth of a new era in human technology

Artificial Intelligence is the science and engineering of creating intelligent machines, especially intelligent computer programs. It's the endeavor to build systems that can perform tasks that typically require human intelligence.

Learning

Systems that can learn from experience and adapt to new circumstances

Language

Understanding and generating human language for communication

Patterns

Recognizing complex patterns in data that humans might miss

Decision Making

Solving problems and making decisions based on available information

The Goal of Artificial Intelligence

The ultimate goal is to create tools that can augment human capabilities and help us tackle challenges too complex or vast for the human mind alone.
